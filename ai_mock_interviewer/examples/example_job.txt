Senior Software Engineer - Backend
TechCorp
San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)

About TechCorp:
TechCorp is a leading technology company building innovative solutions for enterprise customers. We serve over 10,000 businesses worldwide and process billions of transactions annually.

About the Role:
We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our backend team and help build scalable, reliable systems that power our core products. You'll work on high-impact projects, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and mentor other engineers.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable backend services and APIs
- Build and maintain microservices architecture handling millions of requests
- Optimize database queries and improve system performance
- Collaborate with frontend engineers, product managers, and designers
- Participate in code reviews and maintain high code quality standards
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to team growth
- Participate in on-call rotation for production support

Required Qualifications:
- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in one or more backend languages (Node.js, Python, Java, Go)
- Experience with distributed systems and microservices architecture
- Deep understanding of databases (SQL and NoSQL)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with container orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Knowledge of caching strategies (Redis, Memcached)
- Experience with message queues (RabbitMQ, Kafka)
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience leading technical projects
- Startup experience

Technical Stack:
- Languages: Node.js, Python, TypeScript
- Databases: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis
- Cloud: AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda)
- Tools: Docker, Kubernetes, GitHub Actions
- Monitoring: DataDog, Sentry
